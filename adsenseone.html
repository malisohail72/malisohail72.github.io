FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: Business Income Tax kia hai?

A: Business Income Tax woh tax hai jo kisi karobari shakhs ya company ki salana amadni par lagaya jata hai. Yeh tax Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) kay qawaneen kay mutabiq wasool kia jata hai.

Q: Sales Tax ki sharah Pakistan mein kia hai?

A: Pakistan mein sales tax ki sharah mukhtalif ashya aur khidmat par munhasir hai. Aam tor par yeh 17% hai, lekin kuch ashya aur khidmat par yeh sharah kam ya ziyada ho sakti hai. FBR ki latest guidelines se maloomat hasil karna zaruri hai.

Q: Withholding Tax kyu kat'ta hai?

A: Withholding tax payments par kata jata hai taake income tax ki collection ko yaqeeni banaya ja sake. Yeh tax mukhtalif payments par lag sakta hai, jaise ke contractors ko payments, salaries, aur rent wagera.

Q: Business Advance Tax kyu zaruri hai?

A: Business Advance Tax salana income tax ki adaaigi ko asaan banane ke liye hai. Yeh tax saal ke douran qiston mein jama karaya jata hai, taake saal ke aakhir mein bari raqam ada na karni pade.

ℹ️ General Information

Tax Year: Pakistan mein tax year aam tor par 1 July se 30 June tak hota hai.

Filing Deadline: Business income tax returns file karne ki aakhri tareekh aam tor par September 30 hoti hai, lekin FBR is mein tabdeeli kar sakta hai.

Tax Identification Number: Pakistan mein har tax dene wale ke paas National Tax Number (NTN) hona zaruri hai.

Important Note: Yahan diye gaye calculators sirf andaze ke liye hain. Sahih tax calculations aur filing ke liye, Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) Pakistan ki official guidelines ya kisi qualified tax professional se rabta karen.
