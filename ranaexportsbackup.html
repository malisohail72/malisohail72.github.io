FINANCIAL FRAMEWORK

Flexible Payment Solutions

We offer secured transaction methods tailored to your volume, region, and relationship history.

MOST USED

Standard Commercial

Telegraphic Transfer (T/T)

The industry standard balancing risk for both parties. Ideal for new partners and standard FCL orders.

  • 30% Advance to start production
  • 70% Balance on Scanned B/L
  • Fast release of original docs
  • Low bank charges

High Volume Trade

Letter of Credit (L/C)

Bank-guaranteed security for large contracts exceeding $50k USD. Maximum safety for the buyer.

  • 100% Irrevocable L/C at Sight
  • Must be from Top 50 World Banks
  • Zero risk for buyer's capital
  • Best for Government Tenders

Strategic Partner

Cash Against Documents (CAD)

Reserved for long-term clients with a verified history of 3+ successful transactions.

  • 0% Advance required
  • Payment via Bank Collection (DP)
  • Flexible cash flow for buyer
  • Requires Credit Insurance approval

Express & Samples

100% Advance Payment

Designed for sample approval, air-freight consignments, or small LCL orders under $5k.

  • Instant Production Start
  • No documentation delays
  • Cheapest option (Zero Bank Fees)
  • Priority Dispatch status
