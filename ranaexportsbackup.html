Is private labeling available?

Yes, we offer full OEM/ODM services. We can print your brand logo on packaging for Rice, Spices, and Sports goods.

Can I request a mixed container?

Absolutely. We specialize in consolidation. You can mix spices, rice, and salt in a single 20ft or 40ft container.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product. Generally: 1 Ton for Spices, 20ft Container for Rice, and 500 Pcs for Sports goods.
