FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: Budget Planner kaise istemaal hota hai?

A: Budget Planner mein apni maheena aamdani aur kharchon ki tafseelat darj karen, aur phir 'Calculate Budget' par click karen. Yeh aapko aapke kharchon ka tafseeli tajzia aur ek chart dikhayega.

Q: Kya main apne budget ko save kar sakta hoon?

A: Filhaal, yeh feature dastiyab nahi hai. Lekin aap apne browser mein data save kar sakte hain.

Q: Kya yeh calculator sirf Pakistani rupay mein kaam karta hai?

A: Jee haan, yeh calculator sirf Pakistani rupay (PKR) mein kaam karta hai.

Q: Kya main apne budget ka print out le sakta hoon?

A: Jee haan, aap apne browser ke print option se budget ka print out le sakte hain.
