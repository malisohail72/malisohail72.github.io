Budget Planning FAQ

Q: Emergency fund ki zaroorat kya hai?

A: Emergency fund 3-6 mahine ke kharchon ko cover karne ke liye zaroori hai taake aap financial emergencies handle kar saken.

Q: Savings goal kaise set karen?

A: Apne mahana kharach ka analysis karke aur realistic targets set karte hue savings goals tayyar karen.

Q: Qarz se kesy chutkara paen?

A: Debt snowball ya avalanche method istemal karte hue aur mahana budget mein qarz ki adaygi ko priority den.

ℹ️ Financial Planning Tips

50/30/20 Rule: Apni income ko 50% needs, 30% wants, aur 20% savings mein divide karen.

Emergency Fund: Kam az kam 3 mahine ke expenses ko cover karne wala emergency fund rakhen.

Debt Management: High-interest qarzon ko jald se jald ada karna behtar hai.
