Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes. If you don't find your answer here, feel free to contact us.

We provide comprehensive assistance for various visa categories, including student visas, work permits (H-1B, Express Entry, Skilled Worker), investor visas (EB-5, Start-up), family reunification visas, and tourist visas for popular destinations like the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Europe, and Singapore. Our services are tailored specifically for Indian citizens, addressing common challenges they face in the immigration process.

To improve your Canada Express Entry score, focus on enhancing your language proficiency (aim for CLB 9 or higher in IELTS/CELPIP), gain additional work experience in your field, obtain higher education qualifications, secure a provincial nomination (adds 600 points), or obtain a valid job offer from a Canadian employer. Our consultants can assess your specific profile and suggest targeted improvements to maximize your CRS score.

Our initial consultations are completely free. For comprehensive services, our fees depend on the complexity of your case, destination country, and specific services required. We offer transparent pricing packages with detailed service breakdowns and will provide a customized quote after assessing your needs. Government visa fees are separate and paid directly to the respective authorities. We also offer flexible payment plans for major immigration applications.

Indian IT professionals have excellent opportunities in countries like the USA (H-1B visa), Canada (Express Entry, Global Talent Stream), Australia (TSS visa), UK (Skilled Worker visa), and Germany (EU Blue Card). We help with optimizing your resume for international standards, connecting with potential employers, preparing for technical interviews, and navigating the specific work visa requirements of each country. Our network includes IT recruiters specializing in placing Indian talent abroad.

Indian students can access numerous scholarships including Chevening Scholarships (UK), Fulbright-Nehru Fellowships (USA), Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships, Australia Awards, Commonwealth Scholarships, and country-specific programs like DAAD (Germany) and Orange Tulip Scholarship (Netherlands). Many universities also offer merit-based scholarships specifically for Indian students. Our education counselors can help identify scholarships matching your academic profile and guide you through the application process to maximize your chances.
