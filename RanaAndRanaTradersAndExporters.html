Quality Assurance

We follow strict QA: ISO-style inspection checklists, third-party lab testing, HACCP for food products, and traceability from farm to port.

Pre-shipment inspection

Random sampling, moisture & purity tests, packaging integrity checks.

Third-party labs

Certifications and test reports from accredited labs on request.

Traceability

Batch tracking, harvest dates, and chain-of-custody records for high-value consignments.

Our Loyalty Program

Volume-driven benefits: tiered discounts, priority production slots, extended payment terms, and dedicated account managers.

  • Bronze: 0–10 MT / year — standard rates & quarterly support
  • Silver: 10–50 MT / year — 3% discount & faster lead times
  • Gold: 50+ MT / year — custom pricing, priority dispatch, and annual reviews

Registrations & Trade Compliance

We maintain registrations with SECP, FBR (where relevant), TDAP & exporters' associations. We prepare export docs: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, Bill of Lading/AWB, and Inspection reports.

Helpful documents we provide

  • Commercial Invoice & Packing List
  • Certificate of Origin (Chamber of Commerce)
  • Phytosanitary / Health Certificate (for food/agri)
  • Third-party inspection & lab reports
  • Export contracts & insurance documentation
