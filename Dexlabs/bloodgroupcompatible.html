Blood Group Compatibility

Find out which blood types can donate to and receive from each other

Understanding blood group compatibility is crucial for safe blood transfusions. This chart shows which blood types can donate to and receive from other types.

Key Information

  • Universal Donor: O- is the universal donor as it can be given to any blood type.
  • Universal Recipient: AB+ is the universal recipient as it can receive from any blood type.
  • Rh Factor: The "+" or "-" in blood types refers to the Rh factor. Rh- can only receive Rh- blood, but Rh+ can receive both.
  • Always verify compatibility with medical professionals before any transfusion.

Note: This information is for educational purposes. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.
