Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes. If you don't find your answer here, feel free to contact us.

We provide comprehensive assistance for various visa categories, including student visas, tourist visas, work permits, family reunification visas, and business visas for popular destinations like the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Schengen countries, and more. Please contact us with your specific needs.


Processing times vary significantly based on the destination country, visa type, time of year, and individual applicant profile. While we can provide estimates based on current trends during your consultation, embassy/consulate processing times are ultimately beyond our control. We focus on submitting complete and accurate applications promptly to minimize delays.


Our service fees depend on the complexity of the case, the destination country, and the specific services required (e.g., application filing only, full consultation, document review). We offer transparent pricing and will provide a detailed quote after an initial assessment of your needs. Government visa fees are separate and paid directly to the respective authorities.


No reputable consultant can guarantee visa approval. The final decision rests solely with the immigration authorities of the destination country. Our role is to maximize your chances of success by ensuring your application is complete, accurate, meets all requirements, and is presented professionally. We leverage our expertise to guide you through the process effectively.

Required documents vary widely. Generally, you'll need a valid passport, application forms, photographs, proof of financial means, travel itinerary (like flight bookings or hotel reservations), and supporting documents specific to the visa type (e.g., university acceptance letter for student visas, employment contract for work visas, invitation letter for visitor visas). We will provide a detailed checklist tailored to your specific application.
