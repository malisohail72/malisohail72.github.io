United Kingdom Visa Information

Information as of April 2025. Always verify details on the official GOV.UK website.

Visit Visas

Standard Visitor Visa

For tourism, family visits, business meetings, short studies (under 6 months).

Max Stay: Up to 6 months per visit.

Cost: £127 (for 6 months validity)

Covers tourism, visiting family/friends, business activities (meetings, conferences), short-term study (courses under 6 months), transit, or receiving private medical treatment (specific rules apply).

Requires proof of funds, intent to leave UK, accommodation plans.

Long-term Standard Visitor

For frequent visitors. Valid for 2, 5, or 10 years.

Max Stay: Up to 6 months per visit.

Cost: £475 (2 years), £848 (5 years), £1,059 (10 years)

Visitor Visa (Private Medical)

For receiving private medical treatment.

Max Stay: Up to 11 months.

Cost: £220

Visitor Visa (Academic)

For academics on sabbatical, research etc.

Max Stay: Up to 12 months.

Cost: £220

Transit Visas

For passing through the UK en route to another country.

Types: Direct Airside (£39), Landside (£70).

Cost: £39 (Airside), £70 (Landside)

Study Visas

Student Visa

For higher education courses longer than 6 months.

Cost: £490 (outside UK) + IHS*

*IHS: £776 per year (paid upfront).

Requires Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from a licensed sponsor university/institution.

Requires proof of funds for tuition & living costs (£1,334/month London, £1,023/month elsewhere, up to 9 months).

English language proficiency required (typically B2 level for degree courses).

Covers Bachelors, Masters, PhD etc. Specific course level affects CAS requirements but not the visa type/fee itself.

May allow part-time work during term-time.

Child Student Visa

For children aged 4-17 studying at an independent school.

Cost: £490 (outside UK) + IHS*

*IHS: £776 per year (paid upfront).

Requires CAS from the school.

Proof of funds and parental consent needed.

Short-term Study Visa (English)

For English language courses between 6 and 11 months.

Cost: £214

Cannot work or extend stay. Must leave after course.

For courses under 6 months, apply for Standard Visitor Visa.

Work Visas

Skilled Worker Visa

For individuals with a job offer in an eligible skilled occupation from a Home Office-approved sponsor.

Cost (Outside UK):

  • Up to 3 years: £769 + IHS*
  • Over 3 years: £1,519 + IHS*

Cost (Job on Salary List):

  • Up to 3 years: £590 + IHS*
  • Over 3 years: £1,160 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Requires Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) from employer.

Job must meet minimum skill and salary levels.

English language requirement (B1 level).

Can lead to settlement (ILR) after 5 years.

Requires proof of maintenance funds (£1,270) unless exempt.

Health and Care Worker Visa

For qualified doctors, nurses, and other health/adult care professionals with a job offer from NHS, NHS supplier, or adult social care.

Cost (Outside UK):

  • Up to 3 years: £304
  • Over 3 years: £590

No IHS payable. Reduced fee.

Requires CoS from eligible employer.

Must meet specific job and salary requirements (£29,000 minimum generally, or going rate if higher, subject to exceptions).

English language requirement.

Fast-track entry, can lead to settlement.

Requires proof of maintenance funds (£1,270) unless exempt.

Global Talent Visa

For leaders or potential leaders in academia/research, arts/culture, digital technology.

Cost: £205 (with prior approval/endorsement), £766 (without approval/dependants) + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Requires endorsement from a designated competent body.

Flexible - no job offer or sponsorship needed.

Can lead to settlement.

Graduate Route Visa

For international students who recently graduated from a UK university.

Cost: £880 (Apply *inside* UK) + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Allows graduates to work or look for work for 2 years (3 years for PhD).

Must apply from within the UK before current Student visa expires.

Does not require sponsorship.

Does not directly lead to settlement but can switch to other routes like Skilled Worker.

Temporary Worker Visas

Various routes for short-term work (Creative, Charity, Religious, Seasonal, Govt Exchange, Intl Agreement, Youth Mobility).

Cost: £319 + IHS* (most routes)

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront). YMS IHS may differ (£776/yr). Seasonal may be exempt.

Specific eligibility criteria for each route.

Usually requires a Certificate of Sponsorship (except YMS).

Typically do not lead to settlement (Graduate route is separate).

Youth Mobility Scheme (YMS) is for young people (18-30/35) from specific countries.

Business / Investment Visas

Innovator Founder Visa

To set up and run an innovative business in the UK.

Cost (Outside UK): £1,274 + Endorsement* + IHS**

*Endorsement Fee: £1,000 (payable to endorsing body).

**IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Requires endorsement for a business idea that is innovative, viable, and scalable.

No minimum investment fund needed, but must show sufficient funds.

English language requirement (B2).

Can lead to settlement after 3 years.

Replaced the Start-up and previous Innovator visas.

Requires maintenance funds (£1,270).

Global Business Mobility Visas

Routes for overseas businesses transferring staff or expanding to UK (Senior/Specialist Worker, Graduate Trainee, UK Expansion Worker, Service Supplier, Secondment Worker).

Cost (Outside UK):

  • Senior/Specialist (up to 3 yrs): £769 + IHS*
  • Senior/Specialist (over 3 yrs): £1,519 + IHS*
  • Other GBM types: £319 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Specific requirements vary by route (e.g., salary, existing employment length).

Requires sponsorship from the UK entity.

Generally temporary routes, do not lead directly to settlement (except potentially switching).

Expansion Worker is for setting up the first UK branch.

Requires maintenance funds (£1,270) unless exempt.

High Potential Individual (HPI) Visa

For recent graduates from top global universities to work or look for work.

Cost: £880 + Qualification Check* + IHS**

*Qualification Check: £210-£252 approx.

**IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Must have graduated from an eligible university within the last 5 years.

No job offer needed.

Valid for 2 years (3 for PhD).

Does not lead directly to settlement.

Requires maintenance funds (£1,270).

Scale-up Worker Visa

For talented individuals recruited by a UK scale-up company.

Cost: £880 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year (paid upfront).

Requires sponsorship from a licensed scale-up sponsor.

Job must meet skill and salary requirements.

Offers more flexibility after initial 6 months.

Can lead to settlement.

Requires maintenance funds (£1,270) unless exempt.

Family Visas

Family Visa (Partner/Spouse)

To join a partner or spouse who is British or settled in the UK.

Cost (Outside UK): £1,938 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035/year (adult), £776/year (child).

Applies to spouses, civil partners, unmarried partners (2+ years cohabitation), fiancés.

Requires proof of genuine relationship.

Minimum income requirement for the UK sponsor (£29,000 rising further - check current threshold).

English language requirement (A1 initially, higher for extensions/settlement).

Leads to settlement after 5 years (or 10 years route).

Fiancé(e) visa granted for 6 months to marry/enter partnership, then switch inside UK.

Family Visa (Child)

For a child to join a parent(s) in the UK.

Cost (Outside UK): £1,938 + IHS*

*IHS: £776 per year.

Child usually under 18.

Depends on parents' immigration status.

Requires proof of relationship and suitable care arrangements.

Family Visa (Parent)

For a parent to join a child (under 18) in the UK.

Cost (Outside UK): £1,938 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year.

Parent must have sole or shared responsibility for the child.

Child must be British/settled or have lived in UK for 7 years.

Stringent requirements regarding relationship and ability to maintain oneself.

Adult Dependant Relative

For adults needing long-term care from a relative in the UK.

Cost (Outside UK): £3,413 + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year. Fee reduced if relative has protection status (£424).

Very strict requirements.

Must prove need for care due to age/illness/disability that cannot be met in home country.

UK relative must be able to support financially without recourse to public funds.

Other Visas / Routes

UK Ancestry Visa

For Commonwealth citizens with a grandparent born in the UK.

Cost: £682 (Other visa fee) + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035 per year.

Must be 17 or over, able and intending to work.

Proof of ancestry required.

Granted for 5 years, can lead to settlement.

Requires maintenance funds.

British National (Overseas) - BNO Visa

For Hong Kong BNO citizens and their close family.

Cost (Outside UK): £193 (30 months), £268 (5 years) + IHS*

*IHS: £1,035/year adult, £776/year child.

Allows work and study.

Route to settlement after 5 years.

Settlement (Indefinite Leave to Remain - ILR)

Allows living, working, studying in UK indefinitely.

Cost (Apply in UK): £3,029 (most routes)

Eligibility usually after 5 years on qualifying routes (e.g., Skilled Worker, Family, Ancestry).

Requires passing Life in the UK test and English language test (B1).

Must meet residency requirements.

British Citizenship (Naturalisation)

Becoming a British citizen.

Cost (Apply in UK): £1,605

Usually eligible after 12 months with ILR.

Requires passing Life in the UK test, English language test, meeting residency and good character requirements.
