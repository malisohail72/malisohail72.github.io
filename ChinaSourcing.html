Your comprehensive guide to sourcing products from China. Explore our resources to navigate the complexities of manufacturing, quality control, and logistics.

Guangdong Province

Known as the manufacturing hub of China, especially for electronics, toys, and textiles.

Key Cities:

  • Shenzhen: Electronics, tech hardware.
  • Guangzhou: Apparel, auto parts, trade fairs.
  • Dongguan: Furniture, machinery, electronics components.

Zhejiang Province

Famous for small commodities, hardware, and textiles.

Key Cities:

  • Yiwu: World's largest small commodities market.
  • Ningbo: Port city, industrial goods, auto parts.
  • Hangzhou: Textiles, e-commerce hub (Alibaba HQ).

Jiangsu Province

Strong in chemicals, machinery, electronics, and textiles.

Key Cities:

  • Suzhou: Electronics, textiles, manufacturing.
  • Wuxi: Textiles, electric motors, software.
  • Nanjing: Chemicals, electronics, automotive.

Shandong Province

Key for agriculture, food processing, tires, and machinery.

Key Cities:

  • Qingdao: Port city, home appliances, tires, textiles.
  • Jinan: Heavy trucks, machinery, food processing.

Fujian Province

Known for footwear, tea, ceramics, and electronics.

Key Cities:

  • Xiamen: Port city, electronics, stone products, tea.
  • Fuzhou: Textiles, chemicals, plastics.
  • Quanzhou: Footwear, apparel, ceramics.
