FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: Stock Market Capital Gains Tax kia hai?

A: Stock Market Capital Gains Tax woh tax hai jo stocks ki kharid o farokht se hone wale munafa par lagaya jata hai.

Q: Property Capital Gains Tax ki sharah Pakistan mein kia hai?

A: Pakistan mein property capital gains tax ki sharah property ki muddat aur amadni par munhasir hai. FBR ki latest guidelines se maloomat hasil karna zaruri hai.

Q: Savings Account Profit Tax kyu kat'ta hai?

A: Savings account profits par tax kata jata hai taake income tax ki collection ko yaqeeni banaya ja sake. Yeh tax Islamic aur conventional dono savings par lag sakta hai.

Q: Bond Investment Profit Tax kyu zaruri hai?

A: Bond investment profit par tax lagana zaruri hai, yeh tax bond se hone wale munafa par lagaya jata hai.

Q: Mutual Fund Profit tax kaise calculate hota hai?

A: Mutual Fund profit tax mutual fund ki kharid o farokht se hone wale munafa par lagaya jata hai, iski sharah mutual fund ki qisam par munhasir hai.

ℹ️ General Information

Tax Year: Pakistan mein tax year aam tor par 1 July se 30 June tak hota hai.

Filing Deadline: Investment tax returns file karne ki aakhri tareekh aam tor par September 30 hoti hai, lekin FBR is mein tabdeeli kar sakta hai.

Tax Identification Number: Pakistan mein har tax dene wale ke paas National Tax Number (NTN) hona zaruri hai.

Important Note: Yahan diye gaye calculators sirf andaze ke liye hain. Sahih tax calculations aur filing ke liye, Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) Pakistan ki official guidelines ya kisi qualified tax professional se rabta karen.
